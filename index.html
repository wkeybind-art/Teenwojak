Teen Wojak

About

Teen Wojak is a character project based on real teenage life. He scrolls TikTok, procrastinates homework, listens to music, and sleeps way too much – just like most teens

The project captures that everyday vibe: memes, late nights, random motivation, and figuring life out one step at a time

Teen Wojak is simple, relatable, and real – that’s the idea
